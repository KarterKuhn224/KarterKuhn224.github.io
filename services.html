FAQ

Do I need any special tools?

No. A power strip and gaffer tape are recommended to keep cables tidy and safe.

Where should I place the scoreboard?

Beside the bench or scorer’s table, angled slightly toward center court to maximize readability.

How do I keep it looking clean?

Wipe the face with a dry microfiber cloth after each use; avoid liquids near power connections.
