About MoveScore

MoveScore is a portable scoreboard concept built for reliability, fast setup, and clear visibility. The design emphasizes clean typography and straightforward operation so crews stay focused from warmups to the final buzzer.

Designed for tournaments, rec-league nights, and practice scrimmages—where quick changeovers and clear sightlines matter most.
