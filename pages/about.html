About MoveScore

MoveScore is a portable scoreboard concept focused on reliability, fast setup, and clear visibility. The design emphasizes clean typography and straightforward controls so game operations stay smooth from warm-ups to the final buzzer.

Whether you’re running a tournament, a rec-league night, or a practice scrimmage, the layout and guidance are built to keep crews moving and fans informed.
